Seminar Intro

In the MSCS program in UCI, we are required to take the Seminar course for 3 quarters. Each seminar has totally different content about different CS fields. The seminar will be held by many famous scholars and professors. They will show what they are doing and what the state-of-art work of certain field is. At the end of the quarter, we have to write 3 report about the seminar. So, in this blog, I will post several seminar notes.

I have to note that for most of the speech, I cannot understand it well, besides, I may make some mistakes in the notes. After all, that’s the outcome of famous professor with many years’ hard work.

I am very happy to receive your e-mails if you can talk the relevant topic or point out my mistake. Feel free to send my e-mail.
